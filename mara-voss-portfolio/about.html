About Mara Voss

Mara Voss is a fictional digital archivist and interactive media designer based in Port Alder, Oregon.

She is especially interested in digital preservation, interactive storytelling, and accessible web design.

Mara believes that technology should help people understand historical materials without removing them from their original context.

Background

Mara grew up in a coastal town where her mother worked at a local history museum and her father repaired old books.

Her early exposure to photographs, letters, maps, and damaged books encouraged her to study how digital tools can preserve cultural memory.
